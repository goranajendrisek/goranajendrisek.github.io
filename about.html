About me

Focus on the science. I’ll handle the deliverables.

Are you a Medical Director, PI, or Scientific Lead with an overwhelmed team?

You can remain dedicated to high-level strategy and research while I provide the expert support needed to edit, revise, or develop your publications and conference materials from the ground up.

Why collaborate with me?

Hi, I’m Gorana Jendrisek, PhD. I am a biomedical professional with extensive experience in high-impact research. Having completed my PhD and Postdoctoral tenure at the European Institute of Oncology, I have mastered the entire publication lifecycle, from initial evidence synthesis and data interpretation to manuscript drafting, navigating the peer-review process, and final submission.

I specialize in content transformation. Whether you need a complex clinical study transformed into a compelling congress presentation or a rough data set developed into a high-impact paper, I bridge that gap with scientific precision. I utilize professional tools like BioRender and Adobe Illustrator to ensure your data is as visually engaging as it is scientifically rigorous.

Strengths & Expertise

  • Scientific Rigor: I bring the precision of a PhD in Systems Medicine to every project, ensuring content is both accurate and structurally sound.
  • Evidence-Based Narratives: I specialize in synthesizing complex biological data from vast sources into clear, authoritative stories (check my review article).
  • Proven Visual Excellence: I have designed and presented high-quality materials for 11 national and international conferences, recently winning a Best Poster Award in 2025.
