About me

Hi, I’m Gorana Jendrisek, PhD. I am a biomedical scientist and medical communicator with a deep background in high-impact oncology research. Having completed my PhD and Postdoctoral tenure at the European Institute of Oncology (IEO), I have mastered the entire publication lifecycle: from initial evidence synthesis to navigating the complexities of peer review.

I specialize in scientific storytelling. Whether you need to translate complex clinical data into a compelling congress presentation or transform a raw data set into a high-impact manuscript, I bridge the gap between academic depth and clear, audience-centric communication.

Focus on the strategy. I’ll deliver the science.

Are you a Medical Director or Scientific Lead managing a high-volume portfolio?

I provide the expert support needed to develop, edit, and refine your publications and conference materials from the ground up, ensuring your team stays focused on high-level strategy.

Why collaborate with me?

  • Scientific Rigor: I bring the precision of a PhD in Systems Medicine to every project, ensuring content is medically accurate, fair-balanced, and structurally sound.
  • Professional Standards: I maintain active membership in EMWA and am ICH-GCP certified (2026), ensuring my work follows international ethical guidelines and industry best practices.
  • Evidence-Based Narratives: I specialize in synthesizing complex biological data from 300+ sources into clear, authoritative stories.
  • Visual Storytelling: I design and present high-impact materials for international conferences, using visual narratives to make complex data intuitive and engaging, as recognized by a Best Poster Award in 2025.
