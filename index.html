Portfolio

Publication: Beyond the membrane: rethinking EGFR signaling in physiology and cancer

In this first-author publication, I led the writing, peer review, and submission processes. I performed rigorous evidence synthesis across multiple papers to reconcile scientific discrepancies and revised the full text and technical figures for final publication.

Publication: A tripartite organelle platform links growth factor receptor signaling to mitochondrial metabolism

This study advanced the understanding of the molecular mechanisms behind EGFR non-clathrin endocytosis. As part of my PhD research, I performed experiments demonstrating that the non-clathrin pathway's impact extends beyond EGFR to other receptors like MET.

Publication: Inflammatory modulation of the response of bronchial epithelial cells to lipopolysaccharide with pretreatment by montelukast

During my Master's project, I investigated whether montelukast pretreatment modulates inflammatory responses in bronchial cells. As first author, I led the experimental work, writing, and editing, overseeing the peer-review process until publication in a scientific journal.

Conference Support & Coverage: The 4th ENABLE European PhD and Postdoc symposium 2021 Milan

As a member of the Scientific Organizing Committee, I contributed to the event's organization, from choosing the theme to finalizing the official conference newsletter. Within the Scientific Communication subgroup, I managed digital content for international participants and coordinated between internal and external teams to ensure the event's successful execution.

PhD Thesis: Regulation and physiological role of non-clathrin endocytosis of the epidermal growth factor receptor

My PhD thesis explored the regulation and physiological role of epidermal growth factor receptor (EGFR) non-clathrin endocytosis. I designed and executed complex experiments to validate biological hypotheses, requiring rigorous scientific logic and multidisciplinary troubleshooting to achieve reliable results.

Proposal writing: 3-year AIRC fellowship

I wrote a competitive 5-page research proposal and secured a 3-year AIRC fellowship funded by "Acqua Vitasnella", focusing on cancer impact. The proposal featured a detailed research plan, Gantt chart with milestones, and contingency strategies based on preliminary data and established scientific references.

Event Coordination & Digital Support: Belgrade International Molecular Life Science Conference for Students

As a member of the Organizing Committee, I managed attendee registration and provided direct support for participant inquiries. Furthermore, I maintained active digital communications by updating the official conference website and social media channels to ensure a seamless event experience.
